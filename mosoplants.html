MoSoPlants

MoSoPlants was an online multiplayer game developed for Nokia N900 devices for the purpose of exploring non verbal communication between teammates with a common goal.
3 teams of 4 people each were competing for 3 days in order to develop the best strategy of planting trees in a virtual field so that they gain more points for their team. Players in the same team were not allowed to talk or write to each other, but they should rather form a common strategy based on the layout of the field at each point in time.
