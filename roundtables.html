Roundtables

Roundtables is an iPhone app developed as a team project during the Ubiquitus Computing class at University of Houston. Its aim is to facilitate group studying by making the creation, edit and search for group study events very easy and intuitive. It is also integrated with Twitter and Facebook, thus making the events accessible to students without the app.

Get it on the App Store or check out the project page.
